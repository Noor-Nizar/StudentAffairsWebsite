For Special programs Each student must register in the groups specified of his/her majour group, otherwise their registration will be deleted For example, the student who want to attend in Bio G1, then he/she must register in only groups B1, B2, or B3
All students registered in courses during the summer semester (in particular Graduation Purpose courses and Office-based courses) are requested to contact the instructors of these courses to determine the requirements of the courses and set the dates for the midterms. Already some instructors have scheduled the midterms for their courses. It is the responsibility of the students to contact their instructors. Note that the final exams will be scheduled during the period from 18 till 20 September. Good luck to all.
